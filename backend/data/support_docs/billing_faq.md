# Billing FAQ

## Plans and pricing

- **Basic**: $9.99/month, single user, community support only.
- **Pro**: $29.99/month, up to 10 team members, priority email support.
- **Enterprise**: custom pricing, unlimited team members, dedicated support and SLA.

## Payment failures

If a card payment fails, the system retries automatically after 3 days, then again
after 7 days. After the second failed retry the invoice status becomes "overdue"
and the account is flagged for downgrade in 14 days if unresolved. Customers can
update their payment method any time from Billing > Payment Methods, which
triggers an immediate retry.

## Proration

Upgrading mid-cycle is prorated: the customer is charged the difference for the
remaining days in the current billing cycle. Downgrading mid-cycle takes effect
at the start of the next billing cycle; no partial refund is issued for a
downgrade.

## Invoices

Invoices are generated on the customer's original signup date each month and are
available as PDF downloads from Billing > Invoice History going back 24 months.

## Escalation criteria

Escalate to a human agent for any billing dispute involving duplicate charges,
since these require manual reconciliation against the payment processor's
records rather than a documented self-service fix.
