# Password Reset

## How to reset your password

1. Go to the login page and click "Forgot password".
2. Enter the email address associated with your account.
3. Check your inbox for a reset link. The link expires after 30 minutes.
4. Click the link and set a new password (minimum 10 characters, at least one number).
5. You will be signed out of all other active sessions automatically.

## Common issues

- **Reset email not arriving**: check the spam/junk folder first. Emails are sent from
  no-reply@supportapp.io. If it still has not arrived after 10 minutes, the account
  email may be typed incorrectly, or the account may not exist under that address.
- **Reset link says "expired" or "invalid"**: reset links are single-use and expire
  after 30 minutes. Request a new one from the login page.
- **"New password does not meet requirements" error**: passwords must be at least
  10 characters and include at least one numeric digit. Special characters are
  allowed but not required.
- **Two-factor authentication (2FA) locked out**: if the customer has 2FA enabled and
  lost access to their authenticator app, password reset alone will not restore
  access. This requires manual identity verification by a human agent and cannot
  be resolved by self-service reset.

## Escalation criteria

Escalate to a human agent if the customer reports their account email itself was
compromised or changed without their knowledge — this is a security incident, not
a routine password reset.
